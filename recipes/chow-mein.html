Chow Mein

The best Chow Mein you'll ever eat!

Chow Mein noodles are a chinese recipe to make the most incredibly juicy and tasty noodles

With simple ingredients you almost definitely do not have, it is super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce

    2. Mix the sauces together
